Register an organisation to manage civil and family law cases

By registering, you will:

  • create an account for your organisation
  • act as the organisation’s administrator

You will then be responsible for inviting other people from your organisation to join the account.

Before you start

You should make sure that nobody from your organisation has already created an account.

If an account already exists, you should ask the organisation administrator to invite you to join.

We’ll approve the registration within 3 working days. You’ll receive an email asking you to finish creating your account.
