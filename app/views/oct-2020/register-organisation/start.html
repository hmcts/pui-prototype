Register an organisation to manage civil and family law cases

This will create an account for your firm and account for you as the organisation's administrator. You will then be responsible for inviting other users from your organisation to join the organisation account.

Ensure that nobody else from your firm has already created an account, if they have, you should ask them to invite you to the organisatio account.

We’ll approve the registration within 3 working days and send you an email asking you to finish creating your account.
